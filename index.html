Kory Clark

Photographer/Videographer & Software Developer

My name is Kory Samuel Clark. I am currently an undergraduate student at Colorado State University majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Global Environmental Sustainability. With these two fields, I plan on utilizing my programming skills in a way to benefit the environment and further progress research regarding sustainable energy.

Outside of software development, I have a strong passion for photography. Most of my work includes portraits, astrophotography, landscape and time lapses. However, I have worked with High Plains Video Production for the past year and have filmed school plays, weddings, and motivational speakers. I have a sufficient understanding on how to use Adobe Photoshop, Bridge, Premier, and Illustrator and utilize all these tools when editing photos and film. I am always eager and ready for new experiences and cannot wait to see what the future holds.
